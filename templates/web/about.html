About Us

Moon Villas are budget friendly villas made affordable to people for a better lifestyle at the heart and soul of Wayanad, Kalpetta. This exclusive project is proudly presented by Moon Developers in collaboration with Jay n Jay Hoarding Company pvt ltd and Stylex Doors with a revolutionary thought of upgrading the life style and living perspectives of people with budget friendly villas without any compromise in quality of living as a social responsibility and commitment towards the society.

Our Vision

The vision of Moon Villas remains to provide proper housing and vacation experience to people at a very affordable price and to construct a happy living space for its customers at Ponnada, 4kms away from Kalpetta town
